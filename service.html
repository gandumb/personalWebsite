STEM Club

Before leaving for Texas A&M I created, and co-managed a STEM Club at Trent Middle School with the Librarian Mrs. Lassley.

We did bi-weekly mini-project meets on various fields in STEM and hosted speakers twice a school year. We also did one large project every semester where the students were organized into teams and would work in and out of meetings to complete the project.
